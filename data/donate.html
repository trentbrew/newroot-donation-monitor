Donate

We appreciate you.

Your donation supports NewRoot's life-changing mission impacting thousands of educators and students in Chicago Public Schools and districts across the nation through transformative social-emotional learning and restorative justice training.

Thanks to two generous foundations, any new or increased annual donation will be matched up to 200%!

NewRoot is a tax-exempt, charitable organization incorporated under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. All contributions, grants and bequests are tax deductible. The privacy of NewRoot’s donors is important to us. NewRoot Learning Institute does not share or sell donor’s personal information with anyone else, nor will we ever send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations.
